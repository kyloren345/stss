About present day AI

You probably know about ChatGPT one of the most sophisticated AI language models in the entire world. Basically this means that ChatGPT is practically the only machine of its calibre to exist, so far. However, this is only scratching the surface of ChatGPT's calibre, ChatGPT is also a Machine Learning GPT (Generative Pre-Trained Transformer) that uses user input to help itself learn even more, because for example if it says something incorrect, then the user will probably say that what it said was wrong therefore being able to correct itself with somewhat of a saved memory of its mistakes and what it really was. Also ChatGPT uses the entirety of the internet to generate answers for your questions leading to the possibilities that it could be incorrect, this is why there many people at OpenAi that are constantly reducing the parameters for mistakes and increasing the parameters that lead to a correct response. Machine Learning in and of itself is hard to even incorporate into a simple AI machine, the reason for this is because that Machine Learning can take months even years to fully get the true potential out of a machine because its like when you have to learn to do things you've never done before, ChatGPT has been around for over 3 Years and you can tell that those 3 Years were put to possibly forcing ChatGPT to make mistakes so they can remove those parameters while also forcing correct answers to increase the parameters that lead to that.
